Boosts

LEARN MORE

Boosts are BNB Chain ERC-1155 NFTs that increase your staking rewards in different ways once applied.

Boost Types:

  • Time Boost
    This Boost type extends your NFT pool's staking period, providing you with additional money-making time depending on the Boost's power.
  • Profit Boost
    This Boost type increases the interest your earn on your staked NFTs by a number of percentage points depending on the Boost's power.
  • Team Boost
    There's 10 levels to this Boost type - the same as there's Leader Levels. You get a new one each time your team leader hits a new level. You can also obtain one from another person. The Boost increases interest on your NFTs and staking period duration simultaneously.

How do I take the leader's reins?

Expand your structure inviting more quacky friends!

  • Team Boosts unlock for all the leader's team members, helping increase their NFT pools' profitability and staking period duration.
  • You can claim each Leader Level Team Boost only once
  • If you missed out on claiming the Team Boost of a given Leader Level before your team leader reached a new one, you only can claim the new level Team Boost.
  • A newly formed team adopts its new leader's Boost LVL. Their Boost LVL must be higher than the previous leader's one.
  • You can apply Team Boosts to your own staked NFTs, sell them on a DEX, or give them to your friends.
